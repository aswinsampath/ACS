English Language Requirement

Most of the universities require all the students to prove the required level of English

When you make an application in a student visa category that requires you to demonstrate your English language ability, you must show that you have passed an appropriate test.

IELTS & TOEFL are the two well recognised English language examinations which are accepted by most of the universities. Even there is a English language requirements for immigration category for Canada, Australia & UK.

Education Loan

Most of the Indian banks provide loan facilities for the students depending on their circumstances. Basic requirements to apply for education loan are as follows:

  • Should be Indian national

  • Should be 18 years old

  • Should have confirmation of admission by overseas university / college

Secondly you need to demonstrate that you will return back the money to the bank, for this you need to submit the following:

  • Residence Proof

  • Family Income proof

  • Property Registration Papers

  • Property Valuation Papers

  • Certified copy of examinations passed & mark-sheets

  • Fee instalment schedule as given to student by institution

You should know the time taken for the release of loan because different countries follow different requirements to prove the financial ability at the time of Visa application. We recommend you to approach the bank in which you or your parents already have bank account.

The banks generally grant education loans for Tuition fees, Library fees & Living expenses.
