About Us

Salokya Technologies

Salokya technologies is a company that caters to the needs of Security and Surveillance. Our focus areas are products and solutions based on Artificial Intelligence(AI), Machine learning(ML), Internet of Things (IOT) and Blockchain in Defence sector.

Through our new camera we can better scan for license plates on cars, run facial recognition to search for potential criminals or missing people, and automatically detect suspicious anomalies like unattended bags in crowded venues. Hikvision claims they can now achieve 99% accuracy with their advanced visual analytics applications.

The systems use AI to perform tasks like facial recognition, license plate reading and unattended bag detection but that video processing has traditionally taken place on a centralized hub or in the cloud. Our product processes the information within the cameras themselves, this makes the process faster and cheaper.
